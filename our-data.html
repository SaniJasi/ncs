Our Data

The Initial Vanguard Study (IVS) of the National Children's Study ran from January 2009 to September 2010. Using a household enumeration approach to recruitment, pregnant and pre-conception women were enumerated, screened, and enrolled from seven primary sampling units (PSUs): 1) Duplin County, NC; 2) Brookings County, SD; Yellow Medicine, Pipestone, and Lincoln Counties, MN; 3) Montgomery County, PA; 4) Orange County, CA; 5) Queens County, NY; 6) Salt Lake County, UT; and 7) Waukesha County, WI. All SAS datasets from the IVS, which are available in the Researcher Portal to users with approved data requests, are documented in the NCS IVS Pilot Data User Manual. Researchers can also explore the metadata for data collection activities and data elements by using the Protocol Browser and the Variable Locator.
